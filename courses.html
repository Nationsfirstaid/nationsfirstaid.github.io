What Is:

CPR Level A covers adult-only CPR, choking, and circulatory emergencies. Includes AED use.

CPR Level C includes adult, child, and infant CPR techniques, choking, two-rescuer CPR, and AED use.

Blended Learning combines online and in-person instruction. Online component must be completed before the in-class session.
